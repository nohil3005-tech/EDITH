# How to Install EDITH Freelancer Bridge in Google Chrome

To sync your Upwork/Fiverr notifications and incoming messages directly to EDITH, follow these 3 simple steps:

1. **Open Extension Settings**:
   - Open Google Chrome.
   - Go to `chrome://extensions/` by typing it in the address bar.
2. **Enable Developer Mode**:
   - Look in the top-right corner of the Extensions page.
   - Toggle the switch that says **Developer mode** to ON.
3. **Load the Extension**:
   - Click the button in the top-left corner that says **Load unpacked**.
   - Select this folder: `c:\1\EDITH\chrome-extension`
   - Click **Select Folder**.

---

### Sync & Test Controls:
- Click the puzzle icon in Chrome and **pin** the **EDITH Freelancer Bridge**.
- Click the extension icon. It will open a popup window showing a green status dot: **Connected to local EDITH backend**.

#### 1. How to Sync Upwork/Fiverr Jobs (Discover Job Feed):
- Open Chrome and browse to your Upwork job feed (e.g., `https://www.upwork.com/nx/search/jobs/`).
- The extension automatically parses all visible jobs on the screen and sends them to EDITH in the background!
- **Quick Test**: Click **Simulate Discover Job Feed** in the extension popup. Open EDITH, click **Freelance Studio** in the sidebar. You will see 2 brand new matching jobs populated instantly in the **Discover Jobs** tab!

#### 2. How to Process Accepted Proposals:
- Click **Simulate Proposal Acceptance** in the extension popup.
- In the EDITH app sidebar, click **Notifications**.
- You will see the **Upwork Proposal Accepted!** alert. Click **Accept Contract** next to it, and EDITH will dynamically initiate the **Task Processor** execution flow on that project!
